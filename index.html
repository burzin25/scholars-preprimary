About us

At Scholars’ Preschool, we believe the earliest years deserve the most care, attention, and intention.

Our high teacher-to-student ratio and small class sizes allow our educators to truly know each child and give personal attention to their learning style, confidence, and needs.

Because we focus only on Playgroup, Junior KG, and Senior KG, our space and time are fully dedicated to early childhood development — the stage where habits, curiosity, and communication skills are formed.

Over the years, our students have built strong foundations that helped them excel later in school and beyond, pursuing opportunities across India and the world.

Programs
Playgroup

Playgroup

A gentle and joyful first step into school life. We focus on comfort, routine, social interaction, and learning through play.

  • Separation confidence + emotional security
  • Motor skills through games, music, and movement
  • Early communication: songs, stories, speaking practice
  • Play-based learning to build curiosity and attention
Junior KG

Junior KG

Strong foundational learning with structure—without pressure. Children build language, confidence, classroom habits, and early academic skills through activities.

  • Phonics foundation + vocabulary building
  • Pre-writing strokes + fine motor strengthening
  • Number sense, patterns, and basic concepts
  • Group learning, sharing, and communication skills
Senior KG

Senior KG

School-readiness done the right way: reading readiness, confident speaking, writing practice, and independence—so children enter Grade 1 prepared and excited.

  • Reading readiness: phonics + blending
  • Writing practice: letters, words, formation
  • Early math: counting, comparison, basic operations concepts
  • Confidence, independence, and strong classroom discipline
