About us

At Scholars’ Preschool, we believe the earliest years deserve the most care, attention, and intention.

Our high teacher-to-student ratio and small class sizes allow our educators to truly know each child and give personal attention to their learning style, confidence, and needs.

Because we focus only on Playgroup, Junior KG, and Senior KG, our space and time are fully dedicated to early childhood development — the stage where habits, curiosity, and communication skills are formed.

Over the years, our students have built strong foundations that helped them excel later in school and beyond, pursuing opportunities across India and the world.

Programs
Playgroup

Playgroup

A gentle and joyful first step into school life. We focus on comfort, routine, and social interaction.

  • Separation confidence + emotional security
  • Motor skills through games and music
  • Play-based learning to build curiosity
Nursery

Nursery

A year of exploration and expression. We bridge the gap between free play and structured learning through creative activities.

  • Pre-reading: Recognizing letters and sounds
  • Creative expression through art and clay
  • Social skills: Sharing, turn-taking, and teamwork
  • Basic logic and environment awareness
Junior KG

Junior KG

Strong foundational learning with structure. Children build language, classroom habits, and early academic skills.

  • Phonics foundation + vocabulary building
  • Pre-writing strokes + fine motor strengthening
  • Number sense, patterns, and basic concepts
Senior KG

Senior KG

School-readiness: reading readiness, confident speaking, and independence—preparing children for Grade 1.

  • Reading readiness: phonics + blending
  • Writing practice: letters and word formation
  • Confidence and strong classroom discipline
